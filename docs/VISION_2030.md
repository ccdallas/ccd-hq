# CCD Intelligence OS™ — Vision 2030 Blueprint

## Vision Statement
To become the premier mission-driven intelligence platform for healthcare cybersecurity leaders, clinical AI risk strategists, and medical device security architects worldwide.

## Who We Serve
* Healthcare CISOs & Cybersecurity Executives
* Medical Device Security Engineers & IoMT Risk Analysts
* Clinical AI Risk & Regulatory GRC Directors
* Healthcare Strategy & Federal Cybersecurity Consultants

## Product Pillars
1. **Mission-Centric Graph:** Every contact, meeting, research asset, and vulnerability links back to a Mission Node.
2. **Patient-Impact Awareness:** Clinical weighting algorithm prioritizing patient safety above all else.
3. **Evidence-Based Decision Engine:** AI recommendations grounded in live database state with confidence scoring and evidence trails.
4. **Extensible Plugin Ecosystem:** Modular architecture enabling specialized domain packs (IoMT, Clinical AI, Conference Command).
