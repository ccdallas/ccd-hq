# Universal Mission Model & Patient Impact Layer

Every entity in CCD-HQ™ (Contacts, Research, Meetings, Opportunities, Content) links back to a **Mission Node**.

## Patient Impact Scale
* **Critical:** Direct impact on patient safety, clinical device operation, or hospital care delivery (e.g., IoMT vulnerability, ransomware risk).
* **High:** Clinical operations, governance, or healthcare GRC strategy.
* **Medium:** General technical infrastructure or platform improvements.
* **Low:** Administrative, branding, or operational tasks.
